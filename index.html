Who are we ?

We are five students from IESEG School of Management, working with UNICEF and united by our conviction to help children in Vietnam, a country severely affected by floods and storms. Our mobilization means taking action for the future of these children, by protecting and informing them.

Our project

This initiative aims to strengthen resilience to climate change in Cao Bang province, where thousands of families are particularly vulnerable. Specifically, the project will train 200 local leaders and 200 teachers, while raising awareness among nearly 15,000 children and adolescents about climate issues and disaster management. It will also contribute to strengthening protection systems for over 1,000 children and their families. By participating in this fundraising campaign, we hope to help finance concrete actions that will empower younger generations to better protect themselves and take action in the face of climate challenges.

Let's take action in Ciao Bang province, where climate change is disrupting children's lives. Participating means taking action for their future. Every donation, however small, can change lives.

This project not only helps the population of Vietnam, it also bring people together.
